## What Rallyo does

Rallyo is a social companion app that connects people nearby for real-life activities and casual meetups. Unlike dating apps or big public event platforms, Rallyo is built around personal plans — small, spontaneous, everyday moments created by regular people.

A user creates a plan ("looking for someone to play badminton Saturday morning in Andheri") and other nearby users who want to do the same thing can join. It is hyper-local and activity-first, not profile-first.

## Core use cases

- Finding a workout buddy or sports partner nearby
- Solo traveller looking for a local companion in a new city
- Someone bored at home wanting to join a nearby casual activity
- Finding a coding partner for pair programming or hackathons
- Exploring food scenes, restaurants, or street food with someone
- Attending cultural events, concerts, or exhibitions with a companion
- Practising a hobby (dance, photography, swimming) with like-minded people

## Key features (planned)

- Create personal activity plans with time, location, and description
- Discover nearby plans on a feed or map view
- Share photos from activities
- Friend connections and profile management
- In-app messaging between matched companions
- Interest-based filtering

## Target audience

- Age: 18–32 primarily
- People who feel socially isolated or want more real-world connections
- Solo travellers, newcomers to a city, remote workers
- Active individuals (fitness, sports, outdoors)
- Urban users in Indian metros: Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad
